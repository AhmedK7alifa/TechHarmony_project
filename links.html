About The Project

TechHarmony is a comprehensive e-commerce platform showcasing premium Chinese technology products. The website features responsive design, interactive product showcases, brand comparison tools, and a modern user interface. It highlights products from leading Chinese brands like BYD, Huawei, DJI, and Xiaomi with detailed specifications, pricing, and shipping information.
